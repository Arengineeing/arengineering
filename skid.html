SKID MOUNTED RO PLANTS

Introduction

Skid Mounted Reverse Osmosis Plants are designed and built to deliver water suitable for diverse utilization purposes and various industrial process requirements. They are ideal for small hotels, schools/ institutions, nursing homes, commercial establishments and light industries.
