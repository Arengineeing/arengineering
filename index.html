Water Softening Plant

Water Softening Plants : Water is considered ‘hard’ when it has excessive levels of calcium and magnesium dissolved in it. Hard Water can lead to the following Water problems :

  • Scale build-up in pipes, resulting in decreased flow rates
  • Scale build-up on heating and cooling systems, leading to increased electricity costs and breakdowns.
  • White film on glass surfaces and staining on Water taps

Equipments & Spares Of Machines

BELT FILTER PRESS

The belt filter is an industrial machine used for solid/liquid separation processes, particularly the dewatering of sludges in the chemical industry, mining and water treatment.

CLARIFLOCULATORS

Clariflocculator is a combination of flocculation and clarification in a single tank. It has two concentric tanks where inner tank serves as a flocculation basin and the outer tank serves as a clarifier.

GRAVITY BELT THICKENER

Gravity belt thickeners are primarily used for sludge volume reduction prior to digestion, dewatering or disposal. They are a continuously operating machine which thicken sludge by  gravity on a revolving porous filter belt.

SPRIAL / SCREW CLASSIFIER

A Spiral Classifier is a machine that is primarily used to classify the slimes (fines) from a coarser, sandy sized material. It has a inclined trough with one or two spirals revolving slowly and free from touching the sides or bottom of the tank

TRASH CLEANING MACHINE

Trash Rack Cleaning Machine (TRCM) is used to reliably remove debris (branches, trunks, leaves, grass, bamboo etc.) coming with the water & getting struck on the  Trash Racks and blocking the flow of water to ensure unobstructed flow of water to the turbines

TRASH RACK SCREENS

These are heavy structures of Intake systems and pump houses. The basic function of the intake structure is to help in safely withdrawing water from the source over predetermined pool levels and then to discharge this water into the withdrawal conduit (normally called intake conduit), through which it flows up to water treatment plant.
